My Passion: Water Skiing

Ever since I was a little kid my family has gone out boating as much as possible! Since I was 5 years old I have been waterskiing, and have been slalom skiing since I was 10. It has been a huge passion of mine and I wanted to share it with all of you.

 

How to Ski

  1. Here are a few steps to get started
    • Buy a boat or be friends with someone who has a boat
    • Put on your lifejacket, gloves, ski, and jump into the water
    • Have someone toss you the rope and drive the boat
    • When you're ready to go, say hit it
    • Bend your knees, lean back, and hold on for dear life
    • Go from side to side, eventually fall into the water
